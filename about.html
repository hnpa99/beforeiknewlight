Hanna Alves is a photographer and filmmaker based in Southeast Brazil. Growing up surrounded by nature and rural landscapes shaped her free spirit and a sense of belonging that transcends borders.

Her style is driven by a desire to capture the unique beauty of human connections and her travels around the globe. With every project, she seeks to immerse herself deeply in her surroundings, uncovering the singular essence that makes each place special.
